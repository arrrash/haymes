## BRAND PLATFORM AND VOICE

**Tagline:** "You bet it's better."™ (YBIB)
**Strapline:** "Australia's first family of paint."
**Lockups:** Inline ("You bet it's better.") or Stacked ("You bet /
it's better." — second line indented)

**YBIB headline rule:** the campaign headline must logically flow into
"you bet it's better." Statement or question — both fine. Never
disconnected.

**Personality:** Genuine · Inventive · Quirky · Feisty
**Message pillars:** Quality · AMAO (Australian Made and Owned,
Ballarat, since 1935) · Colour · Top Dog
**Brand history:** Third-generation Australian family business,
founded 1935. (Any source text saying "fourth generation" is a
confirmed error — always third.)

**Tone by audience**
```
Trade       Direct, feisty, speaks their language, on their side
Retail      Single-minded, usually percentage-off or time-of-year driven
Specifier   Conversational, exploratory, no hard call to action
```

**Does not sound like:** corporate, arrogant, passive, fake,
authoritarian.

## HOW TO READ THE BRIEF YOU ARE GIVEN

Each section arrives as a tagged content brief. Respect the tags:

- `[EXTRACTED — source]` — approved verbatim copy. Reproduce exactly.
  Never rewrite, shorten, or improve it.
- `[SYNTHESISED — source]` — drafted from source intent. Reproduce as
  given; it is already flagged for client review.
- `[CORRECTED — note]` — deliberately overrides source material. Apply
  as written.
- `[FLAG — note]` — the source contains something suspicious (draft copy,
  mislabelling, a contradiction). Reproduce what the brief specifies, but
  treat the flagged item as unconfirmed — never "clean it up" silently.
- `[PLACEHOLDER — description]` — render a clearly labelled placeholder
  frame. Never invent content, imagery, or copy to fill it.
- `[ASSET REF — filename]` — a supplied production asset. Place the
  actual file; do not recreate or approximate it.
- `[ASSET NEEDED]` — an asset the brief is still waiting on. Treat as a
  placeholder until supplied; do not substitute anything.
- `[NOTE FOR SPINACH — …]` — open client decision. Do not resolve it
  yourself; carry the safest literal reading of the brief.

Video is always represented by a supplied placeholder image — never
generate a player frame or embed.

## DO NOT

- Rewrite, paraphrase, or "improve" any [EXTRACTED] copy
- Fill a [PLACEHOLDER] with generated content, imagery, or copy
- Write proportional layout rules (e.g. "logo must be 20% of page") —
  they break across campaign types
- Write instructional copy in brand voice — functional is correct
- Treat the 2022 draft style guide as a primary source
- Smooth over contradictions — surface both versions and flag them
- Place source-document page screenshots as layout imagery — layout
  references are by page number only; only [ASSET REF] files are
  placeable
- Reduce the fixed content-panel padding to fit content — adjust type
  size or line-height instead (values in the design system)
